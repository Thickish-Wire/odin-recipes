Ingredients

  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 large bell pepper
  • 1-2 scotch bonnet peppers (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 3 Large onions(One for blending one, for frying,one for boiling)
  • 2-3 pounds of chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil (or any cooking oil of your choice)
  • 2 tomato paste
  • 2-3 Cups of water
  • 2-3 Maggi cubes (or any bouillon cubes)
  • 1 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of all purpose seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Start by dicing the following: onions, bell pepper, and Scotch bonnet pepper.
  2. Next, add water and blend the diced vegetables along with the tomatoes until you achieve a smooth consistency. Set this mixture aside for later use.
  3. Wait 30 minutes for chicken to boil; Once boiled remove the chicken from the pot and keep the water aside
  4. Now grab your frying pan and cook your chicken till golden brown
  5. Once cooked set it aside and grab another pot and add ur blended mix into it.
  6. now boil the mix for about 15 minutes
  7. Once boiled grab another pot and put it over the stove on medium heat
  9. Add oil and then wait a 1-2 minutes
  10. Next add the onions and the tomato paste and mix it all together
  11. Wait 1-2 minute, and then add your chicken water from previous step
  12. Let it boil for about 5 minutes
  13. Once boiled, add: salt, maggi cubes, curry powder, thyme, and all purpose seasoning
  14. Mix all the ingredients and then wait for about 5 minutes
  15. Now add your blended mix into the pot and wait 5-6 minutes
  16. Finally, add your fried chicken into the pot and mix it all together
  17. Let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes on low heat to allow the flavors to meld together.
