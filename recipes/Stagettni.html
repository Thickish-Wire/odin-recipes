How to make Stagettini

Stagettini is a delightful pasta dish that combines tender stagettini pasta with a rich and flavorful sauce. This dish is perfect for those who love Italian cuisine and are looking for a comforting meal that is both easy to prepare and delicious. Whether you're cooking for a special occasion or just a weeknight dinner, stagettini is sure to impress with its harmonious blend of textures and tastes.

Ingredients

  • 200g stagettini pasta
  • 2 cups Vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 spoons of Jollof rice spice
  • 1 jollof rice tomato paste and one ordinary tomato paste
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 maggi cube
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 table spoon of all purpose seasoning
  • 2 cups of chicken Broth

Instructions:

  1. First, grab your pot and put it over the oven on medium heat
  2. Add the vegetable oil and let it heat up for a minute
  3. Diced the onion and add it to the pot, let it cook for a few minutes
  4. Add the two tomato paste (jollof flavor and ordinary) and mix it in the pot
  5. Wait for a few minutes and then add your chicken broth
  6. Once the broth is added, add the water and let it boil for 2-5 minutes
  7. Once the water is boiled, Add: the salt, maggi, All purpose Seasoning, curry, Jollof Rice Spice, and thyme.
  8. Mix all the Ingredients and then wait 4-5 minutes.
  9. Add your stagettini pasta and then mix it in the pot
  10. Let it cook for about 10-15 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
