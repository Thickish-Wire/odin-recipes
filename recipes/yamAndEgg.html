How to make Yam and Egg

Yam and egg is a simple yet satisfying meal that blends the earthy richness of boiled yam with the savory goodness of a spiced egg sauce. Popular as a breakfast or light dinner option, this dish is loved for its balance of textures and flavors ‚Äî soft yam chunks paired with fluffy, seasoned eggs. Whether you're cooking for yourself or serving guests, yam and egg is quick to prepare and easy to customize with vegetables, spices, or proteins.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium yam, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 2 or 3 eggs (The number of eggs depends on you)
  • 1 small onion.
  • 1 small tomato.
  • 1 scotch bonnet.
  • 1 tea spoon of salt
  • 1/2 tea spoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 tea spoon of curry powder
  • 2 table spoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 maggi cube

Instructions:

  1. Start by boiling the yam in a pot until it is soft (about 15-20 minutes)
  2. Once the yam is cooked, drain the water and set the yam aside.
  3. Now grab the eggs and crack it into a boil; now add your Salt, black pepper and then finally, mix it all together
  4. Grab your frying pan and put it over the gas on medium heat
  5. Add the vegetable oil and let it heat up for a minute
  6. Diced the onion, tomato, and scotch bonnet and add it to the frying pan
  7. Let it cook for about a minute or so, and then add your curry, and magi to the frying pan and wait for another minute or so.
  8. Now add the egg mixture to the frying pan and let it cook for about 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally to scramble the eggs and mix with the vegetables.
  9. Once the eggs are fully cooked and fluffy, remove the frying pan from heat.
  10. Serve the spiced scrambled eggs over the boiled yam chunks. Enjoy your meal! üçΩÔ∏è
