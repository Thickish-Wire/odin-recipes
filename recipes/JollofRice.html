Jollof Rice Recipe

Hello, Today will be making a popular west african dish called, Jollof rice. A wonderful dish filled with Amazing spices and flavor that always leave you wanting for more. We'll Go through the steps necessary to create the perfect jollof that is prefect for family meals and party celebration. ü•≥üçæ

Ingredients

  • 2 cup of Rice(of your choice)
  • 2 Spoons of butter
  • 2 tomato paste: jollof flavor
  • 2 onions or 1 Large Onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • Jollof rice spice
  • 2 table Spoons of salt
  • 2-3 cups of water or as needed
  • 2 maggie Cubes
  • Topping of your Choice: Beef, Chicken, Pork etc.
  • 2 table spoon of all purpose Seasoning
  • 2 table spoon of curry
  • 2 table spoon of thyme

Instructions

  1. Grab a pot and add your oil and butter and let it steam for a few minutes
  2. Diced your onions and add it to the pot
  3. Add the two tomato paste and mix it in the pot
  4. Wait a few minutes and then then add the necessary water to cook the rice.
  5. Let the water boil for 2-5 minutes
  6. Once the water is boiled, Add: the salt, maggie, All purpose Seasoning, curry and thyme.
  7. Mix all the Ingredients and then wait 4-5 minutes.
  8. Add your rice and then mix it in the pot
  9. Let it cook for about 20-30 minutes on low heat.
  10. Once the rice is cooked, Serve and enjoy your meal üçΩÔ∏è
